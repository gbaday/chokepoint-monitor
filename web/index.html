Gas Chokepoint Dashboard

US gas/LNG equities ranked by composite Sweet Spot score — JKM/TTF/HH spread sensitivity, valuation, growth, technical setup.

Why This Exists

Track the equities most exposed to the structural gas/LNG chokepoint between US Henry Hub and global JKM/TTF prices. Composite scoring lets us rank torque vs. valuation vs. setup in one place.

What To Look For

High Sweet Spot with positive Spread beta + clean balance sheet = directional torque to widening spreads. High Reset + Not Extended = better risk/reward entry.

Best Use

Screener for daily idea generation. Cross-reference Sweet Spot rank, fundamentals, and earnings calendar before sizing. Watchlist below isolates small-caps.

Data Caveat

Z-scores are cross-sectional across this universe (not historical). Bloomberg fields (short interest, days to cover, earnings) require an open Terminal — empty cells show "—".
